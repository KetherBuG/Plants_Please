Fruits and Vegetables

1. Cabbage


Hey we are starting off you with something easy! Cabbages take anywhere from 80to 180 days to grow and can form six heads from one stub. The best time to plant them is in the fall (as they are a cooler vegetable) and grows best in liquid fertilizer. You have to water it 1 to 1.5 inches a day (if no rain). Once harvested you can make many yummy meals!
You can buy the plant at Home Depot for a mere $20: Home Depot Chinese Evergreen
